About Us

AGPT Accounting and Business Consultancy is a general professional partnership officially registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission, established on July 1, 2015. The firm was born from the shared vision of four Certified Public Accountants who had been working independently before coming together. Their goal was to bring accounting and tax services closer to small and medium-sized business owners in the Bicol region. AGPT offers a wide range of services focused on quality, cost-efficiency, value, and client satisfaction. These principles are reflected in their tagline: “Making every business count.” The founders strongly believe that effective accounting and taxation are key to helping businesses achieve both organizational and financial success.
